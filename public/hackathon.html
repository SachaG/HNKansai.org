KansaiHack is a one-day hackathon in Kyoto.
A chance for people all over Kansai to get together and create something new.

  • Register

What?

A hackathon is an event where people form small teams to try and create a new project in a short amount of time.

When?

April 20th, 2013
9 am - 9 pm.

Where?

Vitei, 10F Yasaka Shijo Karasuma Bldg.,
Kankoboko-cho 79 Shimogyo-ku,
Kyoto 600-8009, Japan

Why?

To meet new people, learn new things, and finally work on that project you've always wanted to do.

Who?

KansaiHack is organized by the HNKansai team.

Questions? Ideas? Want to say hi? Contact us.
